Synopsis

In Barbieland, a matriarchal utopia, “Stereotypical” Barbie enjoys a perfect life until she develops flaws and fears about mortality. Weird Barbie advises her to find the child playing with her in the real world. Barbie travels there with Ken, meeting Sasha, a teen who criticizes her, and Gloria, Sasha’s mother, whose drawings caused Barbie’s crisis. Meanwhile, Ken learns about patriarchy and returns to Barbieland, leading the Kens to take over. Barbie, with Gloria, Sasha, and the outcasts, deprograms the Barbies and regains control, vowing fairer treatment for Kens and misfits. Ken learns to seek his own identity. Barbie meets her creator, Ruth Handler, who tells her her story has no set ending. Choosing to become human, Barbie returns to the real world as “Barbara Handler” and attends her first gynecologist appointment.
